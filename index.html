Our Mission

Belong & Begin is a student-led project focused on making Novi High School a more inclusive and welcoming place for all students. We aim to create a facility that serves as both a rest center and a recreational space for individuals with and without disabilities. By modifying classic games for accessibility and providing a safe, supportive environment, we promote relaxation, fun, and socialization for kids and families in our community.
