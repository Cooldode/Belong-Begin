Our Mission

Belong & Begin is a student-led project focused on making our community a more inclusive and welcoming place for all students. We are creating a facility that serves as both a rest center and recreational space, designed for individuals with disabilities and those without, providing safe, fun, accessible activities to foster friendships and growth.

Our Core Values

We are guided by the principles of Inclusion, Accessibility, Community, Play, and Respect. Our mission is to ensure every student feels seen, valued, and included in a space designed to meet diverse needs.
