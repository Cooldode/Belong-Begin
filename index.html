Our Mission

Belong & Begin is dedicated to making Novi High School and its community more inclusive by providing a safe, adaptive space for individuals with disabilities and all students to relax, play, and build meaningful connections.

Our Core Values

Inclusion, Accessibility, Community, Play, and Respect guide everything we build and offer, ensuring every student has a place where they belong and can begin thriving.
